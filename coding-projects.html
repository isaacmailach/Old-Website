Time

The digital art piece, Time is meant to show the repetitive process of growth and decay as time passes and how this affects one's legacy. Time involves a simple animation of a tree growing then dying as a day passes. The animation then repeats every 4 minutes. Nothing remains each loop, showing the lack of meaning in one's life. However, one can leave a message in Time which is reshown each loop. The message becomes a legacy, a source of meaning to the endless cycle. Others can view and be inspired by a message and leave their own message, thus adding to the collective. As Time evolves, more and more messages are displayed. Time was developed for a public display. It is made in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The messages are stored locally, so they will be deleted when the web page is reloaded.
